GAME PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 3/09/2025

Your privacy and data security are critically important to us. We are committed to managing, protecting, and using your personal details with care and transparency.

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") explains what information yaoyituopu Games and its affiliates ("we", "our", or "us") collect, and how that information is used, stored, and safeguarded when you access or use our mobile games distributed through Google Play.

1. Information We Collect

i. Device details

We may collect device and network-related information to ensure stable app operation. Based on your settings, this can include: SDK/API/JS code version, Android ID, browser type, ISP, IP, platform, timestamps, app identifier and version, distribution channel, device identifiers, advertising ID (IDFA/Android), MAC address, IMEI, device brand and model, OS version, session start/stop time, language, timezone, network type (Wi-Fi, 4G, etc.), storage usage, CPU, and battery level.

ii. Advertising ID

We rely on your advertising identifier (Android ID/IDFA) for analytics and ad personalization. This identifier is not tied to sensitive permanent data like IMEI or MAC address.

iii. Third-party SDKs and analytics

Some third-party SDKs integrated into our apps may collect information such as your advertising ID, region, IP, or language. These include services like Google AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, and Firebase. Data handled by these third parties is subject to their own privacy policies.

iv. Information you provide

We also store information you voluntarily provide, including email address, survey participation, feedback, or user-generated content (UGC) submitted under our terms.

v. Users in Designated Countries

For users located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland (the "Designated Countries"), we process data as required under GDPR and related laws, primarily under the legal basis of contract fulfillment.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected is used to: operate, maintain, and improve our services; personalize user experience; monitor and analyze trends; show relevant advertisements; protect against misuse; and comply with applicable laws.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share data with trusted partners and service providers (such as analytics or ad networks) solely for business purposes. Any sharing will follow strict contractual safeguards.

4. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

5. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security technologies and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

6. Third-party Privacy Policies

Our services may contain links or integrations to third-party services. Their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies, including:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Facebook Audience Network: https://www.facebook.com/policies/ads
  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

7. Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to how we process your data. You can also request a copy of your personal data or withdraw consent where applicable.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may revise this Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new “Effective Date.” Continued use of our services means you accept those changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at: game@yaoyituopu.com.
