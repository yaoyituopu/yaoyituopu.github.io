About Us

We create games
for peaceful moments

Founded in 2020, our small team of passionate developers believes that games should be a source of relaxation and joy, not frustration. We focus on minimalist design and intuitive gameplay.

Our mission is to create digital experiences that help people unwind, focus, and find moments of peace in their busy lives through simple, beautiful games.
